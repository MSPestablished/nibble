Scope of work

We're system builders — not your accounting department

Nibble's job ends where your bookkeeper's job begins. That line is intentional — it keeps your numbers owned by you, not locked inside a vendor relationship.

In What Nibble does
Design and configure your chart of accounts across every entity

How a setup runs

Four stages. One system that doesn't break.

Every engagement follows the same sequence — skipping a stage is how multi-entity books end up tangled in the first place.

01Discovery
Map the entities
We document every legal entity, how money and goods actually move between them, and which reports each stakeholder needs to see — before touching any software.
02Build
Configure the system
Chart of accounts, entity hierarchy, intercompany mapping, bank feeds, approval rules, and consolidation logic — built directly inside your chosen ERP.
03Train
Hand the keys to your people
Your bookkeeper or accounting hire is trained on the live system — not a sandbox — so day one of independent use is also day one of real entries.
04Support
Stay reachable, not embedded
We remain available for system questions, edge cases, and periodic health checks. The books stay yours; the system stays sound.

Built for groups

Multi-entity isn't a setting. It's the default.

Most accounting software treats a second entity as an afterthought. We configure the structure on purpose — so consolidation, intercompany transactions, and entity-level reporting work the way your group actually operates.

Entity & group reporting
Run a P&L for one branch or the whole holding structure, without rebuilding reports each time.
Intercompany mapping
